Empowering Safer Workplaces Across South Africa

At BSMT Services, Safety isn’t just gear it’s knowledge. Our Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) Training Division provides certified, competency based training that equips workforces with the essential skills to operate safely and confidently.

We are proud to be QCTO accredited (Quality Council for Trades and Occupations), Ensuring our programs meet the highest standards for occupational training in South Africa.

What We Offer

OHS Compliance Training

Covers legal frameworks, employer duties, and employee rights under South African safety law.

Hazard Identification & Risk Assessment

Teaches proactive risk detection and mitigation strategies.

PPE Usage & Workplace Protocols

Hands on training for PPE selection, application, and maintenance.

Emergency Preparedness

Fire safety, evacuation procedures, first aid, and crisis management.

Sector Specific Modules

Tailored training for mining, construction, manufacturing, municipal teams, and more.
