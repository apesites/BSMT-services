SHERQ AND ISO

SAFE . COMPLIANT . CONFIDENT

Integrated Risk. Sustainable Quality.

At BSMT Services, We engineer systems that safeguard operations, satisfy regulations, and foster continuous improvement. Our SHERQ & ISO Division specializes in building robust frameworks for Safety, Health, Environment, Risk, and Quality, fully aligned with ISO standards, South African legislation, customer standards and international best practice.

Whether you’re seeking certification, compliance, or operational resilience, our team delivers strategic consulting and hands on implementation.

What We Offer

SHERQ Systems Integration

Holistic design and deployment of management systems covering all SHERQ domains.

ISO Implementation & Certification Prep

Support for ISO 9001, ISO 14001,ISO 22000, ISO 45001 gap audits, SOPs, readiness training.

Hazard identificaion and risk assesment

Detailed hazard Identification and Risk Assesment aligned to legislative standards.

Compliance & Legal Audits

Full compliance evaluations under the OHS Act, NEMA, and Environmental By laws.

Internal Auditor Training

Certified programs to upskill staff in ISO and SHERQ system auditing.

Occupational Medical Surveillance

Protect workers health, reduce occupational risks, prevent occupational illnesses and injuries.
